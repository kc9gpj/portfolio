About Me



A lifelong experimenter, tinkerer, and world traveler. Since I was young I have enjoyed building computers, designing radio antennas, and experimenting with technology. I also enjoy camping, hunting, and a variety of nerd culture.



I grew up in the great state of Wisconsin, and I currently live near Kansas City. I graduated from Olivet Nazarene University in Illinois in 2012 with a Bachelor of Science. After that I spent 4 years in the Army as a Logistics Officer. The majority of my service was at Fort Riley, KS and southern Afghanistan.



Full Stack Development utitlizing Python and Javascript along with their associated frameworks and libraries are my main skillset. I am also competant with Devops related tasks.
